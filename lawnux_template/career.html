We Are Excited to Work Together

Lawnux Law and Consultancy Office deeply respects all its lawyers and colleagues and provides an environment where they can develop themselves. It upholds the ethical values of the legal profession to the fullest. Employees of Lawnux Law and Consultancy Office feel the support and trust of the team at all times while also appreciating their individual worth. Lawnux Law and Consultancy Office seeks lawyers, trainee lawyers, and colleagues with excellent legal skills and analytical thinking.

Our recruitment process is interview-focused. Candidates who are deemed suitable after filling out the preliminary application form below or sending their CV to bilgi@lawnux.av.tr will be invited for an interview. The interview process is a re-evaluation of the qualifications stated in the candidate's resume. During this process, there is also a discussion about the working conditions of our office and whether the candidate's expectations match. Lawnux Law and Consultancy Office takes great care in selecting its team members and makes candidates feel special. You will receive either a positive or negative response to your application.

As a trainee lawyer, not having sufficient practical knowledge and feeling like a ‘fish out of water’ during the job application process is the same for every law school graduate. Do not worry, we also graduated from the same faculty with the same concerns. We have not forgotten the trainee lawyer issues we experienced. Therefore, our valuable trainee colleagues working with us should know that you will quickly overcome your practical inexperience with our willingness to share our knowledge and experience. The most important quality we seek during this process is how eager you are to learn information during your faculty years. As long as we see this eagerness and diligence, we promise that you will experience the master-apprentice relationship, which is one of the traditions of our profession, with us.
