OUR MISSION

We ensure the preparation of the legal ground of the fields in which our clients operate, addressing the legal disputes they experience in their daily lives. This removes obstacles and provides legal services to facilitate their activities. With preventive legal services, we identify legal risks that may arise against our clients before a dispute occurs and aim to minimize these risks. We establish all our relationships with the trust required by the legal profession. To strengthen this trust, we work with all our might to resolve legal issues as quickly and accurately as possible.

OUR VISION

‘’A global law and consulting office that helps you take confident steps on the legal foundation of the future!’’ As legal regulations continuously change and evolve with human needs and technology around the world, we follow these changes and developments through the collaboration of our lawyers and consultants across different continents. Thus, we become a global law and consulting office that supports our clients' ventures and needs across continents. Happy clients, happy lawyers who make the client's work easier.

Lawnux Law and Consulting Office provides services aimed at resolving legal disputes in various areas, including Contract Law, Commercial Law, Debt Law, Personal Data Protection Law, Labor Law, and Family Law, as well as Criminal Law, Consumer Law, Administrative Law, and Enforcement and Bankruptcy Law.

Emir, since founding Lawnux Law and Consulting Office, has actively worked in areas such as Criminal Law, Family Law, Consumer Law, Labor Law, Real Estate Law, and Debt Law; and specializes particularly in compliance with the Personal Data Protection Law, Corporate Law, and Contract Law.
He has actively participated in various association and social responsibility activities. Lawnux, who has pointed out the need for improvement in legal education and litigation activities, has spoken at numerous seminars to raise awareness among participants on these topics.
