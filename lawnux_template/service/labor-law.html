Labor Law

At Lawnux, we specialize in providing expert legal counsel and representation in the complex field of labor law. With a deep understanding of the dynamic and ever-changing landscape of employment regulations, we are committed to protecting the rights of both employers and employees. Our team of experienced labor law attorneys is dedicated to ensuring that workplaces adhere to the highest standards of fairness, equality, and compliance with federal, state, and local labor laws.


Our Approach to Labor Law

Labor law encompasses a wide range of legal issues that arise in the workplace, including employment agreements, wage and hour disputes, workplace discrimination, harassment, wrongful termination, and collective bargaining. At Lawnux, we understand the delicate balance that must be maintained between the rights of employees and the needs of employers to manage their businesses effectively.

Our labor law team provides tailored legal solutions that align with our clientsâ€™ goals, whether they are employers seeking to ensure compliance and mitigate risks or employees pursuing justice for unfair treatment. We are committed to promoting productive, respectful, and legally compliant work environments.


Services for Employers

At Lawnux, we offer comprehensive legal services to employers of all sizes and industries, helping them navigate the complex regulations that govern the employer-employee relationship. We assist in developing policies that promote legal compliance and reduce the risk of litigation. Our services include, but are not limited to:

  • Employment Contracts and Agreements: Drafting, reviewing, and enforcing employment contracts, non-compete clauses, confidentiality agreements, and severance packages.
  • Compliance with Labor Laws: Advising on compliance with the Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA), Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA), Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA) standards, and other applicable laws.
  • Workplace Policies and Handbooks: Assisting in the development of employee handbooks, codes of conduct, anti-discrimination policies, and procedures for addressing workplace grievances.
  • Wage and Hour Disputes: Ensuring compliance with wage and hour laws, overtime pay regulations, and employee classification (exempt vs. non-exempt).
  • Workplace Investigations: Conducting internal investigations into claims of harassment, discrimination, or misconduct in the workplace to mitigate legal exposure.
  • Collective Bargaining and Union Negotiations: Providing counsel in negotiating collective bargaining agreements, addressing union disputes, and navigating labor relations issues.
  • Defense Against Employment Claims: Representing employers in disputes involving wrongful termination, discrimination, wage and hour claims, and employment-related litigation before administrative agencies and courts.

Services for Employees

We are equally committed to representing employees who have experienced violations of their labor rights. Our experienced attorneys advocate on behalf of workers who have faced unfair treatment, unlawful termination, or discriminatory practices. Our services for employees include:

  • Discrimination and Harassment Claims: Representing individuals who have been subjected to workplace discrimination or harassment based on race, gender, sexual orientation, age, disability, religion, or other protected characteristics.
  • Wrongful Termination: Pursuing claims for employees who have been wrongfully terminated in violation of employment contracts or as a result of discriminatory practices.
  • Wage and Hour Disputes: Advocating for employees who have been denied overtime pay, minimum wage, or other compensation to which they are entitled under federal and state laws.
  • Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA) Violations: Assisting employees who have been denied their rights to medical or family leave under FMLA, or have faced retaliation for exercising these rights.
  • Retaliation Claims: Representing employees who have been retaliated against for reporting workplace violations, whistleblowing, or participating in protected activities.
  • Severance and Employment Agreements: Reviewing and negotiating severance packages, employment contracts, non-compete agreements, and confidentiality clauses to protect the interests of employees.

Collective Bargaining and Union Representation

The Lawnux labor law team has extensive experience representing both employers and employees in union-related matters. Whether negotiating collective bargaining agreements or defending against unfair labor practices, our attorneys are skilled in handling the complexities of labor relations.

  • For Employers: We provide guidance in negotiating with unions, responding to strikes or labor disruptions, and addressing grievances under collective bargaining agreements.
  • For Employees and Unions: We represent employees in unionization efforts, grievance arbitration, and disputes with employers over contract violations or labor conditions.
